Our Story

Wuxi Dilong Casting Co., Ltd. is located on the bank of the Beijing-Hangzhou Canal, East China, southern Jiangsu Province, Yangtze River Delta Plain, north of the Yangtze River, south of Taihu Lake, known as the "Pearl of Taihu Lake."

  • Wuxi Dilong Casting Co., Ltd has established itself as one of the leaders in the investment casting industry since 1970. We have sustained our successful business by developing high-quality products, providing exceptional services, and delivering on time at a competitive price.
